Our Official Product Range

Comprehensive aluminum and glass architectural systems engineered for maximum durability, aesthetic elegance, and thermal efficiency.

Aluminum Stick Type Curtain Wall

High-performance stick-built glazed curtain wall systems engineered for multi-storey commercial towers.

Aluminum Composite Panel Cladding

Durable 4mm ACP cladding systems with PVDF coating for high weather resistance and modern facade styling.

Aluminum Windows

Thermally broken casement, sliding, and fixed aluminum windows built for residential and commercial use.

Aluminum Doors

Heavy-duty aluminum entrance, swing, and hinged door systems engineered for security and sleek design.

Automatic Sliding Door

Smart automated motion-sensor sliding doors for high-traffic commercial building entrances and plazas.

Aluminum Louvers

Glass Partitions

Skylight Systems

Industry FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Detailed technical & operational answers for developers, architects, and contractors.

We specialize in aluminum stick-type curtain walls, unitized glazed facade systems, structural silicone glazing, and point-fixed spider glass walls. Our systems are engineered specifically to withstand coastal humidity, wind pressures, and thermal expansion across high-rise commercial towers in Karachi and Pakistan.

Yes! We have delivered over 17 landmark commercial tower projects in Bahria Town Karachi (including Takmeel Trade Tower, Hallmark Tower, Madina Icon, Farooq Tower, and Plot SQ-29) as well as DHA Karachi (Z.A Plaza DHA Phase VIII, Plot 5-C DHA Phase VI, and Plot 130-C DHA Phase II).

We install premium 4mm exterior-grade Aluminum Composite Panels (ACP) with PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) fluorocarbon coating. This specification ensures maximum UV stability, fire resistance, weatherproofing, and long-term color retention backed by a 15–20 year guarantee.

Absolutely. Our specialized engineering team develops detailed CAD shop drawings, 3D structural elevation models, and wind-load/seismic calculations for architect approval and municipal building authority compliance before fabrication begins.
